    What Are Structural Observations?
    (TEMPORARILY LIMITED TO CURRENT PROJECTS)

    Whether you are purchasing a new home, selling a home or just need a piece of mind on potential structural issues you notice around your property, we could assist you with the evaluation of the concerned areas and provide recommendations based on our findings.

    If you are in need of a structural observation during the construction of your project and have city or county approved plans, We could assist you with inspecting the work and providing the necessary forms needed to proceed with the construction.
